Grandmaster

Drama | 117 min

Role: Pia Cramling

Description: Set against the backdrop of the late 20th century, this biopic drama tells about Pia Canning, one of the first female chess grandmasters. Her journey is one of trials, mastering the game and the art of breaking patterns.
